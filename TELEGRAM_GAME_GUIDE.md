# Tiger Strike - Telegram Game Guide (Live Build)

Use this as your official player-facing guide for "How to Play" and "What everything does".

## 1) Core Gameplay Loop
1. Move your operator on the map and locate civilians under threat.
2. Escort civilians to the green Evac Safe Zone.
3. Fight tigers by either:
- Capture: safer long-term control, lower aggression spikes.
- Kill: faster clear, but increases aggression pressure more.
4. Use map tools (Alarm, Barricade, Cache), Squad commands, and abilities to stabilize missions.
5. Clear mission objectives to earn cash, XP, perk points, chapter rewards, and pass progress.

## 2) Combat Outcomes
- Capture: best for control-focused play and mission stability.
- Kill: best for emergency threat removal, but can increase jungle aggression and pressure pacing.

## 3) Abilities (Player)
- Scan: reveals/helps locate threats.
- Sprint: movement burst for repositioning.
- Shield: temporary protective bubble for player and nearby civilians.

## 4) Squad Specialists (Shop > Squad)
Unlock rules:
- Story level unlock: Level 15.
- Anytime unlock option: 500 Stars each role.

Roles:
- Tiger Specialist: frontline tiger combat and capture pressure (high HP/armor role).
- Search & Rescue Specialist: civilian collection and evac escort focus.

Current economy:
- Hire each specialist: $100,000.
- Reinforcement bundle (both): $175,000.
- Revive (single): $150,000.
- Revive all downed: $150,000 (flat rule in current build).

## 5) Shop - Weapons
- Tranq Pistol Mk I: tranq sidearm starter, efficient capture setup.
- 9mm Sidearm (Used): low-cost lethal sidearm for fast clears.
- Pump Shotgun: strong close-range lethal burst.
- Tranq Rifle: longer-range tranq precision for safer captures.
- AR Carbine: balanced mid-range lethal control.
- DMR Marksman: long-range high-impact lethal.
- Tranquilizer Launcher: heavy tranq option for high-threat capture control.
- Prototype Rail Rifle: extreme lethal finisher weapon.

## 6) Shop - Weapon Attachments (Deep System)
Optics:
- Holo Optic: more range + faster target pickup, slight jam risk.
- LPVO 1-6x: best long-range precision, slower reload flow.
- CQC Dot: close control boost, weaker long-range confidence.

Tranq Chamber:
- Stable Tranq Chamber: higher tranq pressure, lower lethal throughput.
- Overdrive Chamber: strongest tranq pressure, with recoil/reload tradeoff.

Suppressor:
- Heavy Suppressor: lower signature, but reduced range/damage.
- Light Suppressor: moderate stealth with lighter penalties.

Magazine:
- Extended Mag: larger mag, slower reload and slight jam pressure.
- Quick Mag: smaller mag, faster reload and cleaner feed.

Stock:
- Stability Stock: recoil/reliability up, slight handling tradeoff.
- Light Stock: faster handling, increased recoil.

## 9) Perks (Core XP Perks)
Perks are upgraded with perk points (max rank 6 each, spread-gated for balanced growth).

Hunter perks:
- H_CRIT: increases crit chance.
- H_DMG: increases outgoing damage.
- H_JAM: reduces weapon jam pressure.

Guardian perks:
- G_CIV_GUARD: reduces civilian damage taken.
- G_ARMOR: improves armor efficiency.

Tactical perks:
- T_TRAPS: improves trap effectiveness scale.
- T_RELOAD: improves reload speed.

## 10) Story Meta Progression
### Base Upgrades
- Base Armory: +starting armor in Story missions.
- Logistics Wing: better minimum mission-start supplies.
- Shield Network: +starting shields.
- Endurance Program: lower stamina drain.
- Operations Finance: higher Story payout cash.

### HQ Modules
- R&D Lab: higher Story payout + wider capture research window.
- Medbay: better escort survivability and rescue movement.
- Intel Center: lowers tiger pressure / improves mission stability.
- Armory Deck: stronger starting armor/shields + specialist edge.

### Specialist Perks
Tiger Specialist:
- Tiger Specialist Drill: more damage/HP/armor.
- Capture Tactics: better capture reliability and Story capture window.

Rescue Specialist:
- Escort Formation: faster rescue movement/guiding.
- Civilian Guard Protocol: less tiger damage on rescue unit + nearby civilians.
